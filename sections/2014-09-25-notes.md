Top down design
===============

Algorithm for drawing a house
-----------------------------

1. initial outside shape
    - size
    - roof type
    - door location
        - frame
        - knob
    - window locations
        - outline
        - lines
    - chimney location(s)

2. texturing/colouring
    - materials (e.g. walls, windows)
    - colour (doors, , roof)
    - wall material/brick colour
    - door colour
