Latest commit was basically creating the whole program, since I did most of the threading & sockets biz in an afternoon sprint back in Christmas break. Requires a lot of cleanup, but the core is present and working – surprisingly – without a hitch (even though I don’t actually drop clients at all).

Multithreaded server. Keeps a listener thread for new clients and assigns a thread and handler to each connected client. No idea about implementation of file sending.
